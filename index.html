About Me

Hi. I'm Jehoia Cato. My nickname is Jay.

I was a transcriber and data entry clerk for an I.T. company in Maryland. While transcribing the Scrum meetings, I learned about the software development life-cycle for building websites and applications that meet clients' needs.


I grew an interest in software engineering and the process of coding, testing, and deploying websites and apps. So, I joined Generation USA's full-stack development bootcamp, where I'm currently learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, Bootstrap, Mocha, Git, and much more.


Because I have such a passion for full-stack development, I know I made the right career shift when joining the software engineering field.
